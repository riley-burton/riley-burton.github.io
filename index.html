About

I am a third year PhD student at UMass Amherst studying pure mathematics. My advisor is Kristin DeVleming (UCSD), and while at UMass, I also work with Paul Hacking. I am primarily interested in algebraic geometry, in particular moduli spaces of algebraic varieties.

During Fall 2026, I will be visiting ICERM (Brown University) for the semester program Computations on K3 Surfaces and Related Varieties.
